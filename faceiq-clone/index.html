Workflow

A repeatable analysis cycle

Capture, quantify, prioritize, and re-measure — each loop refines your morphology profile.

  1. 01

    Ingest

    Submit frontal and lateral captures. MorphIndex normalizes lighting and alignment automatically.

  2. 02

    Quantify

    Symmetry, Proportions, Structure, and Expression axes are scored independently, then synthesized.

  3. 03

    Protocol

    Interventions and habits are ranked by projected impact on your weakest metrics.

  4. 04

    Track

    Re-run the cycle, compare sessions, and let MorphAI answer protocol questions along the way.
